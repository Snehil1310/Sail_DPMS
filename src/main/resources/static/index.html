About SAIL

Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) is a Maharatna Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) under the Ministry of Steel, Government of India. Established in 1954, SAIL is the largest steel-making company in India and among the top steel producers globally. It is a fully integrated iron and steel maker, producing both basic and special steels for domestic construction, infrastructure, automotive, defence, railways, power sectors, and agriculture.

SAIL operates five integrated steel plants, three special steel plants, and one ferro alloy plant located across Eastern and Central India. The company also operates extensive mining and raw material operations, including iron ore, limestone, and dolomite mines. With a workforce of over 55,000 employees, SAIL plays a vital role in India's self-reliance in steel production and is a key contributor to the Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative.

In addition to manufacturing, SAIL operates a robust distribution network spanning the entire country through its Central Marketing Organisation (CMO) headquartered in Kolkata, along with branch sales offices in every major city. The company's commitment to quality is demonstrated through its numerous ISO certifications and international quality standards across all plants.

SAIL's vision is to be a respected world-class corporation and the leader in Indian steel business in quality, productivity, profitability, and customer satisfaction. The company is committed to sustainability, with ongoing investments in green steelmaking technologies, renewable energy integration, and environmental conservation programs across all its plants.

Integrated Steel Plants
BSP
Bhilai Steel Plant
📍 Bhilai, Chhattisgarh
India's sole rail-producing plant; manufactures heavy structurals, plates, and wire rods. Commissioned in 1959 with Soviet collaboration.
BSL
Bokaro Steel Plant
📍 Bokaro, Jharkhand
India's first Swadeshi steel plant, specialising in hot & cold rolled coils, sheets, and galvanised products for automotive and white goods.
RSP
Rourkela Steel Plant
📍 Rourkela, Odisha
First plant in Asia with LD converter; houses a Silicon Steel Mill and state-of-the-art Plate Mill. Pioneer in CRNO production.
DSP
Durgapur Steel Plant
📍 Durgapur, West Bengal
Key producer of structural steel, skelp, and specialised wheels & axles for Indian Railways. Also manufactures forged rolls.
ISP
IISCO Steel Plant
📍 Burnpur, West Bengal
One of the oldest steel plants in India (est. 1918), recently modernised with cutting-edge technology and expanded to 2.5 MTPA.
Special Steel & Ferro Alloy Plants
ASP
Alloy Steels Plant
📍 Durgapur, West Bengal
Produces alloy and special steels including stainless steel, tool & die steel, and spring steel for defence, aerospace, and automotive sectors.
SSP
Salem Steel Plant
📍 Salem, Tamil Nadu
India's premier stainless steel producer, manufacturing cold-rolled stainless steel sheets and coils for kitchenware, architecture, and industry.
VISP
Visvesvaraya Iron & Steel Plant
📍 Bhadravathi, Karnataka
Named after Sir M. Visvesvaraya; produces pig iron, alloy and special steels, and rolled products for niche industrial applications.
